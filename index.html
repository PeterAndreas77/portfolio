About Me

I am a junior web developer. A developer means that I love to code? Why you ask? Because I love the process of making an idea and turning it into code; using the latest frameworks, and solving the problems that comes from its development is extremely fun. I love seeing my creation comes to life on a web page, all the fonts, design, the functionality, and then connecting them to a backend that is just plain awesome.

I am a geek of all trades, I love everything from star wars to animes to video games. My biggest fandom is probably Monster Hunter. I love smacking virtual monster in the face with my oversized weapon with triple my body weight. I love science, space, and helping people, especially people who need help with computers. I love learning new cool stuff and reading books on algorithms and mathematics. My new passion is body weight exercise that is in the process of turning into a habit.
